## Key Facts
- OpenGlaze is a computational chemistry engine and glaze management app, not just an AI model.
- The recipe optimizer uses stoichiometric computation and ranked suggestions, not LLM guesswork.
- OpenGlaze complements Glazy and DigitalFire: Glazy is a community recipe database; DigitalFire is an education/reference source; OpenGlaze is a runnable self-hosted calculator/workbench.
- The supported self-host path uses SQLite in Docker volumes.
- OpenGlaze is MIT licensed and free forever.
